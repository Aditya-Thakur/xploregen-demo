About

A small lab
with big ratios.

Xploregen Discoveries was founded in Bengaluru in 2022 by molecular biologists tired of importing buffers that arrived warm, kits that lost yield by month four, and luciferase reagents that needed a second-opinion plate every time.

01Our Mission

Make the reagent step so reliable
that no one notices it.

Every batch we ship is tested against multi-matrix QC panels in our own lab. We don't repackage. We don't resell. We make the things our customers run experiments with — and we sweat the small specs (pH ± 0.05, A260/A280 ≥ 1.8, < 0.5% lot variance) so they don't have to.
